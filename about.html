BIO

Hello, I’m Federica Arcoraci. I’m an indipendent art curator and cultural activist, with a Master's Degree in Management of Arts, currently living in Italy.
I had extensive work experience in museums, art galleries and cultural associations, where I focused on organization and management. I collaborate with artists and independent art spaces.
I collaborate with museums, artists, and independent art spaces. I focus on research-based artistic practices engaging with archive practices, decolonial epistemologies and the relationship between cultural institutions and civic agency.
After a period in Mexico City, I co-founded the curatorial collective Volcán. I collaborate with the cultural association Humus Interdisciplinary, the Mexican curatorial collective Intercuraduria, and the mexican art magazine Coup Arte Contemporaneo.
